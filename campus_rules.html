Campus Rules

Welcome to Woxsen University! Here are some important campus rules:

  1. Follow the dress code guidelines.
  2. Respect your fellow students and faculty.
  3. Keep the campus clean and tidy.
  4. Adhere to academic integrity policies.

Rules for Campus Exit

  • Outing & leave are permitted only during university leave declared for festivals and national holidays (Weekends are not considered university holidays).
  • Emergency leave may be given. What constitutes an ‘emergency’ is defined below:
    • Medical emergency for self, parents, siblings only.
    • For self, a student must first visit Care and Cure and will be referred to hospitals outside if needed.
    • Death of a close relation or sibling who is hospitalized in a serious condition.
    • It does not apply to family celebrations, birthday parties, anniversaries, or farewells to siblings overseas.
    • No argument with the security authorities.

Procedure for Availing Leaves

  • For exiting campus on weekends, the Microsoft Form link given will be open till Thursday at 3 PM where students can put in requests by giving valid details and attaching the prescribed format of the letter of consent written & signed by parents. Approved/Not Approved email will be sent to all students.
  • For parents visiting campus, they should inform the CSO via mail at least 24 hours prior.
